Explore Software Stack
AI Compiler
Our Homegrown AI Compiler lets you build your AI applications in popular languages and AI frameworks to compile them directly to our GPX Processors. Consistently growing support for more neural networks and operators means you have complete control and ownership of your AI models.
Development Libraries
We provide a rich set of development libraries including math libraries (Arithmetic Logic, Compare, etc.), AI Libraries (Convolution, Recurrent, etc.) as well as programming APIs so you can develop, debug, profile and emulate your AI applications with ease.
Sample AI applications
Our SDK includes sample applications and complete training toolchains so you can get started within minutes and experience the magic of on-device AI. These include AI-based facial recognition, voice commands, fall detection, driver monitoring system and more.

About Us

Ambient Scientific:
Democratizing Green AI

We are a fabless semiconductor company, pioneering next-generation AI processors to power computers of the future.
Born in Silicon Valley, Ambient Scientific was created to tackle a daunting problem: free AI from the shackles of the internet and cloud computing. Our team of world-class scientists and experts came together to rethink conventional computing concepts and reinvented Analog Compute technology to achieve the impossible: ultra-low power always-on AI.
